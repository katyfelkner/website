Ideas and Proposals

My goals for my term as president fall broadly into three categories: member experience, professional development, and supporting leaders.

Mental Health Crisis Training

  • Ask exec members, Judicial Chair, Candidate Educators, and Scholarship Chair to complete mental health crisis training within the first month of their terms
  • Next semester, bring in a speaker to offer training to everyone in the chapter
  • Ideas for sources:
    • Free online training from Suicide Prevention Research Center
    • Presentation from University Counseling Center - potentially coprogrammed with OUr Mental Health
    • Mental Health First Aid from the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention

Mental Health Resources Reference Sheet

This was also suggested during goal-setting at retreat. This should be executable withing the first few weeks of my term.
  • Include hotlines, OU resources, local providers, and links to reliable info
  • Hosted on front page of ASK drive, with a bit.ly link for easy access
  • Pin link in actives slack channel, put it in the topic of all groupmes, and include it on the bottom of minutes every week

Monthly Sister Dates

  • This idea has been suggested before, and it's time to implement it!
  • Rules: In groups of 3, do something besides study for at least 30 minutes. You can go to a meeting or talk, get food, or just meet and chat.
  • Pairing: Randomized using this script. Manual post-processing to ensure that bigs/littles and roommates/close friends are not paired up.
  • Accountability: Google Form submitted by one of the group, option to include a picture of the group hanging out for social media!

Sister Tech Talks

At retreat, we discussed in goal setting that we want to see more professional development opportunities. Giving sisters a space to practice presentations was one of the ideas that came up.
  • Recruitment Event: Let's showcase the technical side of our organization in our recruitment! Sisters can sign up to do 5-10 minute talks, and we can have fun science-related trivia and watch cool science videos.
  • In Chapter: I feel like I know my sisters on a personal level, but I don't know what technical work they are doing!
    • Sisters can present over undergrad research, internship projects, class projects, or just topics they like learning about!
    • Fun, low stress opportunity for members, especially younger members, to practice presentation skills in a friendly setting
    • Completely optional and designed to be low-stress
These tech talks will help reaffirm our identity as both a technical and a social organization.

Streamlined Reporting to Exec

Having a faster and more objective process for reporting potential problems to exec will benefit both reporting sisters and the exec board.
  • All reporting for hazing, harassment, risk management, etc. concerns will go through a confidential Google Form.
  • Exec board will read forms and respond within 48 hours.
  • All correspondence regarding reported situations will come from askbeta@gmail.com
    • Keep all exec members in the loop
    • Reduce stress for individual exec members
    • Faster responses when one exec member is out of town
  • After a meeting with the exec board, the reporting sister and the presiding exec member will sign a document that includes what was reported and what actions the parties have agreed should be taken. These documents will be kept in confidentiality by the secretary.

Exec Accountability Plans

  • Inspired by Holmes Leadership Associates Leadership Development Plan.
  • Basically, this is a plan where leaders identify target capabilities, then measure improvements on those targets at regular intervals.
  • Implementation for ASK:
    • Focused on action rather than leadership skills
    • Each exec member will set SMART goals for their term
    • Exec will discuss progress toward goals, rate their individual progress, and report to the chapter once a month. Minutes from these discussions will be open record and available on the drive.

Exec Board Transparency

Exec meetings are already open and we do post minutes. However, we can do a better job of keeping the chapter updated.
  • Send out exec minutes with meeting minutes every week.
  • Include a link to exec minutes in the weekly minutes
  • In chapter, President's report will include a short (2-3 minute) summary of what happened in that week's exec meeting
